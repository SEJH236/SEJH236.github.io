Millennium Park – Chicago, IL

One of the most iconic spots in Chicago, Millennium Park features public art, gardens, and the famous "Bean" sculpture (Cloud Gate). A must-visit for city lovers.

Address: 201 E Randolph St, Chicago, IL 60602

Contact: (312) 742-1168

Starved Rock State Park – Oglesby, IL

Known for its scenic canyons, waterfalls, and hiking trails, Starved Rock is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts looking for a quick getaway from city life.

Address: 2678 E 875th Rd, Oglesby, IL 61348

Contact: (815) 667-4726

Galena Historic District – Galena, IL

Step back in time with this charming town filled with 19th-century architecture, boutique shopping, and rich Civil War history.

Address: Main Street, Galena, IL 61036

Contact: (815) 777-9050

Lincoln Park Zoo – Chicago, IL

Located in the heart of Chicago, the Lincoln Park Zoo is a free, family-friendly destination with a variety of animal exhibits and beautiful gardens.

Address: 2001 N Clark St, Chicago, IL 60614

Contact: (312) 742-2000

Rockford Speedway – Rockford, IL

For racing enthusiasts, Rockford Speedway offers thrilling motorsports events throughout the summer and fall seasons.

Address: 1288 IL-75, Rockford, IL 61102

Contact: (815) 633-1500

Lake Geneva – Lake Geneva, WI

Just north of the Illinois border, Lake Geneva is a popular destination for water sports, dining, and scenic lake views.

Address: 201 Wrigley Dr, Lake Geneva, WI 53147

Contact: (262) 248-4416

Shawnee National Forest – Harrisburg, IL

Located in Southern Illinois, Shawnee National Forest offers stunning natural beauty, hiking trails, and opportunities for wildlife viewing.

Address: 50 Highway 145 S, Harrisburg, IL 62946

Contact: (618) 253-7114
