Garden of the Gods – Shawnee National Forest

This natural wonder offers stunning rock formations, hiking trails, and panoramic views, making it one of the most photographed locations in Illinois.

Address: Herod, IL 62947

Contact: (618) 253-7114

Rend Lake – Benton, IL

Popular for fishing, boating, and camping, Rend Lake is a great spot for nature lovers and families looking to relax and unwind outdoors.

Address: 11712 E. Wayne Fitzgerrell Rd, Benton, IL 62812

Contact: (618) 439-3177

Cahokia Mounds – Collinsville, IL

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Cahokia Mounds preserves the remains of an ancient Native American city and offers educational exhibits and trails.

Address: 30 Ramey St, Collinsville, IL 62234

Contact: (618) 346-5160
