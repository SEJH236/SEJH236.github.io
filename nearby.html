St. Louis Gateway Arch – Missouri

An iconic monument with tram rides to the top, museum exhibits, and riverboat cruises. Just across the Mississippi River from Illinois.

Address: 11 N 4th St, St. Louis, MO 63102

Contact: (877) 982-1410

Mammoth Cave National Park – Kentucky

The world's longest known cave system offers guided tours, hiking, camping, and wildlife viewing in a beautiful forested area.

Address: 1 Mammoth Cave Pkwy, Mammoth Cave, KY 42259

Contact: (270) 758-2180
